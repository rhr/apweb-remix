HERBARIUM SEQUENCE
[Back to Top]

This sequence is constructed so that as many terminals (families) as possible that are adjacent in the phylogeny of angiosperm families are adjacent in the sequence, given the constraints of the topology of this tree. This has been done by making the tree as pectinate as possible (see Introduction), i.e. as many taxa as possible are either adjacent branches along pectinations or are sister taxa. The sequence is then read left to right from the tree.
